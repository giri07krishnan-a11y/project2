About Electric AI

Know Your Bill Before It Knows You

Electric AI is an intelligent energy companion that forecasts your electricity bill using machine learning — analyzing your consumption patterns, household size, and daily usage habits to give you an accurate prediction before your actual bill arrives.

🎯

Our Objective

Empower households to plan finances better by removing billing surprises through predictive analytics.

💰

Real Benefits

Avoid billing shocks, identify wasteful consumption early, and make informed decisions to reduce costs.

🧠

Why AI?

Traditional estimations ignore behavioral patterns. Our linear regression model learns from real consumption data to deliver precise, personalized forecasts.

Why Machine Learning?

01Learns from thousands of consumption data points
02Accounts for household size & daily usage hours
03Continuously improves prediction accuracy
04Delivers instant, data-driven forecasts
