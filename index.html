About Electric AI

Know Your Bill Before It Knows You

Electric AI is an intelligent energy companion that forecasts your electricity bill using machine learning by analyzing your consumption patterns, household size, and daily usage habits to give you an accurate prediction before your actual bill arrives.

⚡

Our Objective

Empower households to plan finances better by removing billing surprises through predictive analytics.

⚡

Real Benefits

Avoid billing shocks, identify wasteful consumption early, and make informed decisions to reduce costs.

⚡

Why AI?

Traditional estimations ignore behavioral patterns. Our linear regression model learns from real consumption data to deliver precise, personalized forecasts.

About the AI Model

🤖

Model Used

Linear regression trained on historical consumption patterns.

📊

Dataset

Household bills, seasonal usage, family size, and appliance intensity.

⚙️

Technologies

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Chart.js, and predictive analytics logic.

🎯

Accuracy

High confidence forecasts with adaptive estimation for monthly usage.
